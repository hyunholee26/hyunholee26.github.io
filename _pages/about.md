I am a Senior Manager at K-water(Korea Water Resources Corporation), also I am currently studying at Arizona State University as a GIS Ph.D Student in Tempe, United States. I am a member of CICI (Cyber Infrastructure and Computational Intelligence) lab advised by [Dr. Wenwen Li](https://search.asu.edu/profile/1978357). 

I am focusing on deep learning for geospatial data, especially satellite data, and its interpretability. Additionally, I am interested in the applications of deep learning for the water resources management. Currently, I am working on waterbody segmentation, including flood, using satellite data based on deep learning models.

Previously, I received a M.S. degree in Computer Sciecne from the KAIST(Korea Advanced Institute of Science & Technology), advised by prof. Kwangyun Wohn.
